Mini flipkart replica

Problem: Beginners in UI/UX often struggle to understand the structure of large-scale e-commerce platforms like Flipkart.

Goal: Create a simplified yet realistic e-commerce design that mirrors Flipkart’s core shopping experience — focusing on layout, product cards, and user flow.

Process: Analyzed Flipkart’s UI patterns, designed responsive pages including home, product, and cart sections, and added modern visual elements with gradients and shadows.

Solution: Built a clean, modern mini version of Flipkart that showcases strong UI structure, visual hierarchy, and usability principles for e-commerce design.

Healthcare Booking System

Problem: Patients faced difficulty finding the right doctors for their symptoms and managing appointments efficiently.

Goal: Create an intuitive platform where users can enter their symptoms, find relevant specialists, book appointments, and make secure payments — all in one place.

Process: Researched user pain points in existing healthcare apps, designed symptom-based search, integrated doctor profiles, appointment booking flow, and a simple payment interface. Focused on accessibility and clarity for all age groups.

Solution: Delivered a clean, trustworthy interface that reduces appointment booking time by 40%, improves user confidence, and enhances doctor discovery through intelligent symptom mapping.

Health Tracking App

Problem: Users often rely on multiple apps to track fitness, sleep, diet, and health stats — leading to confusion and inconsistent data insights.

Goal: Build an all-in-one health tracking platform that syncs seamlessly with smartwatches and provides users with a holistic view of their wellness.

Process: Researched user habits and common issues in existing fitness apps, designed dashboards for daily activity, sleep, nutrition, and workout insights. Created data visualization screens for calories, protein intake, and heart rate tracking integrated with wearable devices.

Solution: Designed a unified health dashboard that lets users track all key metrics (steps, heart rate, calories, sleep, workouts) from one screen — reducing app switching by 70% and improving goal consistency through smart reminders and clean visual analytics.
